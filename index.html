About Us

The idea of FoodRush started when I was planning to start a business of my own. I was doing research about how to start and handle a business. I was observing our local area and noticed that most people in my barangay really like fast food, but the problem is that most of the fast food restaurants are far away. That is when I decided to build this business. I never really thought that I would come this far, but everything went better than expected.
